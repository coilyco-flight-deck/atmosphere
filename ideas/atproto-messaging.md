# atproto-messaging

**What** - atproto messaging. Tangled is what made the whole Atmosphere click for Kai ("Tangled is what got me here"), and the idea of an atproto messaging surface is genuinely appealing. No material use decided yet - the primitive itself is what draws her.

**Why it fits the Atmosphere** - messaging on atproto rides atproto identity. Different apps take different approaches to where the messages live (Germ does DMs, Roomy does group chat with CRDT sync).

**Open questions**

- What it is FOR. The appeal is real, the use case is not pinned down.
- Relationship to [chat](chat.md) - chat is the room-shaped version, this is the messaging-shaped version. They may merge.

Separate but related: Kai plans to adopt **Tangled** itself ("I'm gonna have to tangle") and move work onto it. That is a tooling-adoption move, not an app to build here, but it is the thing that lit the fuse.
