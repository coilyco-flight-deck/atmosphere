# reviews-tracking

**What** - a tracking and reviews app, Letterboxd-for-X shape. Books, movies, games, music scrobbles. Each tracked item and rating is a record (Popsky, Bookhive, Skylights, Teal.fm, Rocksky already do versions of this).

**Why it fits the Atmosphere** - a tracking app is a list of typed records with ratings, which maps cleanly onto a repo. A custom lexicon plus a small App View is the whole thing. This is one of the lowest-effort categories.

**Why it might be for Kai** - she already keeps a games library and reading habits in the vault. An atproto-backed tracker makes that portable and optionally public.

- Which medium first. Games is the one with existing vault data to seed from.
- Overlap with the vault. Is this a new surface or an atproto export of vault data.
