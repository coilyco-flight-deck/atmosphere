# atproto-x-my-streams

**What** - the strategic thread. Not one app, a question: where does AT Protocol intersect Kai's actual professional streams in a way that nobody else is positioned to build? "I feel like there's something there. I gotta think through a little bit."

The four intersections to think through:

- **AT Protocol and developer velocity**
- **AT Protocol and platform engineering**
- **AT Protocol and observability**
- **AT Protocol and agentic programming**

**Why it matters** - the rest of the ideas in this directory are apps. This one is the lens that decides which apps are worth Kai's time specifically. The play is the combination of atproto with the things Kai already has deep range in, not atproto for its own sake.

**Why it fits the Atmosphere** - whichever intersection wins probably becomes an App View, a labeler, or a custom lexicon. Observability of the firehose, agentic agents that read and write repos, dev-velocity tooling on Tangled - all are real atproto surfaces.

**Open questions**

- Which of the four is the sharpest. Agentic programming plus atproto feels live given the lights-out factory work.
- Whether observability of atproto itself (firehose lag, relay health, App View latency) is a product or just a dashboard.
- This needs a real thinking session, not just a seed. Sit on it.

Related: every other file in this directory is a candidate downstream of this one.
