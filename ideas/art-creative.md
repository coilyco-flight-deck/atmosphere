# art-creative

**What** - a creative surface on atproto. Draw-in-browser art boards (PinkSea), custom emoji (Bluemoji), generative or procedural art.

**Why it fits the Atmosphere** - art pieces are blobs plus metadata records. A custom lexicon plus an App View that renders a gallery.

**Why it might be for Kai** - the procedural angle. galaxy-gen is a procedural generator that renders in the browser. A generative-art surface that publishes pieces as atproto records is a real bridge between that work and the Atmosphere.
