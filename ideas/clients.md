# clients

**What** - an alternative Bluesky client. A different front door over the same atproto accounts and posts (Graysky, deer.social, Ouranos all do this).

**Why it fits the Atmosphere** - a client is an App View plus a UI. Same firehose, same repos, different product opinions on layout, muting, feeds.

**Why it might be for Kai** - a client tuned to how Kai actually reads. Topic-segmented by default, wired straight to her custom feeds, phone-first.

**Why it might not** - building a polished social client is a large, never-finished surface. High effort, crowded field.

**Open questions**

- Is this just the consumer end of [topic-feeds](topic-feeds.md) rather than a separate build.
