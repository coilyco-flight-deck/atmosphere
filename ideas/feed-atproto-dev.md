# feed-atproto-dev

**What** - a topic feed for AT Protocol and Atmosphere development. Feed generators, lexicons, Jetstream, PDS hosting, the protocol itself.

**Why it fits the Atmosphere** - feed generator, one of the N topic feeds described in [topic-feeds](topic-feeds.md). This is the highest-volume tech community on Bluesky because Bluesky is its home turf, and it is the exact community atmosphere is being built inside. Doubles as dogfooding.

**Interest signal** - Kai is now actively building atmosphere. The whole repo is the evidence.

**Keyword sketch** - `atproto`, `at protocol`, `feed generator`, `lexicon`, `jetstream`, `bluesky api`, `PDS`, `app view`.

**Tier** - Tier 1 (high interest, high volume). Recommended prototype-first feed: keyword terms are unambiguous so false positives stay low, and the feed visibly fills.
